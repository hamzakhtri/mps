What We Repair

Controller Repairs

Is your controller unresponsive, drifting, or broken? We repair and replace faulty game controllers to restore your gaming experience.

Display Issues

Screen flickering, blackouts, or distorted graphics? We diagnose and repair display issues for a seamless visual experience.

Power Issues

Having trouble powering on your console? Whether it's a faulty power supply or internal issue, we provide efficient power repairs.

Connectivity Issues
