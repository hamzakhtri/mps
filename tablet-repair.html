What We Repair

Screen Calibration

If your tablet’s touch screen is unresponsive or misbehaving, we can calibrate it for accurate touch sensitivity.

Water Damage Repair

Dropped your tablet in water? We offer water damage diagnostics and repair services to save your device and its data.

If your tablet has trouble connecting to Wi-Fi or Bluetooth, we can troubleshoot and fix the issue to restore stable connections.

Whether it’s low or no sound, we can fix your tablet’s speakers or audio system to ensure clear, loud sound again.
